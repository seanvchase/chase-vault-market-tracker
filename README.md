# Chase Vault Market Tracker

Chase Vault Market Tracker is a trading card inventory and market analysis app. It helps users track card values, compare eBay market prices, scan cards using AI, and make buy, sell, or hold decisions based on price trends.

## Planned Features
- AI card photo scanner
- eBay market price lookup
- Inventory and portfolio tracker
- Profit/loss calculator
- Buy, sell, or hold recommendations
- Market movement alerts
